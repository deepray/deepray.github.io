Research interests


  • Conservation laws

    I primarily study sytems of conservation laws such as the Euler equations, and look at various properties like entropy stability, positivity and preservation of analytical properties. I also study the Navier-Stokes system with the aim of designing finite volume schemes using the symmetrization properties.

  • Computational fluid dynamics (CFD)

    I am also involved in the numerical implementation of schemes developed for fluid flows. I code using C++, Fortran, MATLAB and Python, with the aid of visual tools like Paraview, Tecplot and Visit.

  • Optimal control theory

    I am interested in the control and stabilizabilty of systems by considering the linearized model. This includes the evaluation of feedback control and solving estimation problems with partial observations in the presence of noise.
