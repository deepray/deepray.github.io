Hi! I am a postdoctoral researcher in Professor Jan S. Hesthaven's group at EPFL, Switzerland. My research involves the study of numerical techniques for partial differential equations. In particular, I study systems of conservation laws from the point of view of preserving important physical properties, such as entropy stability, at the discrete level.
